
PAGES
-cards (Each card is a row. Click a card to edit. each row has a qty box for each card. you can fill them in and then there is a drop down of decks at top and an add to deck button).
-editCard (all the fields, save button).
-decks (list of decks, you click on a deck to edit it).
-editDeck (list of all cards and qty in deck, can change qty, 0 will remove. Save button).
